About me

Greetings! I'm Hayden, my passion is designing and programming games. Currently pursuing a degree in Games, Interactive Media, and Mobile Technology(GIMM) at Boise State University, my primary focus revolves around programming and level design. With that said I would characterize myself a versatile generalist who can contribute on a little bit of everything. I have a strong commitment to continous learning and feel like I should always be improving or honing my skills. Feel free to explore some of my showcased works or to reach out and contact me.
